Ecuisina

Trade homecooked meals and experience new cuisines locally.








The first scalable food sharing network.



New Experiences

Meet new people and try new foods. Expand your taste of cuisines from around the world that could be right next door! There is always something new to try!




Quality Foods

Trade authentic homecooked meals easily and locally. Homecooked meals are the most authentic a meal can get!




Affordability

Before spending hundreds of dollars on groceries and making meals every day. Create one of your best dishes and trade it away for food that you would like.



Get started trading in no time.



List food and display it at your home or work address.

You can list any food that you have, and it will be display on the map at the locations of your choosing.




Find food in your area.

Use filters on the map to sort food at different locations by distance, cuisine, food types, days you are available to trade, etc.




Trade food that you want

Once you find a food you would like to trade for, you can request for the food item. If the user accepts your request, they will advance the trade.
